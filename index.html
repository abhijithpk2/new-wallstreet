FTX

FTX is a Bahamian cryptocurrency exchange.[2] FTX is incorporated in Antigua and Barbuda and headquartered in The Bahamas.[3][4] As of February 2022, the exchange averages $10 billion of daily trading volume[5] and has over one million users.[6] FTX operates FTX.US, a separate exchange available to US residents.[7] FTX was founded for the purpose of generating as much wealth as possible to be used for charitable causes following the earning to give principle of effective altruism.
